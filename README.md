### 🔍 Project Overview

Transforms raw sales data into an interactive executive dashboard, surfacing monthly revenue trends, top-performing products, regional sales distribution, and channel performance.

### 📖 Project Objectives

Build an executive-level KPI dashboard in Excel
Improve visibility into sales performance across regions and channels
Identify top-performing products and categories
Demonstrate advanced Excel dashboarding and data visualization skills

### 🧰 Tools Used

Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Pivote Charts
Advanced Formulas
Slicers & Interactive Filters

### 📊 Key Dashboards 

1. Executive Revenue Overview
2. Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
3. Top 5 Products Performance
4. Revenue by Region
5. Revenue by Sales Channel
6. Category Revenue  Distribution

### 📌 Highlights 

₦120.1M Total Net Revenue
910 Total Orders Processed
₦132K Average Order Value
East Region – Highest Revenue Contributor
NutriMix Cereal Box 500g – Top-performing Product

### 📈 Insight Generated

Revenue peaked in June 2025 at ₦6.32M
Online was the highest-revenue sales channel
Snacks and Baked Goods were the top-contributing categories
East and North regions showed the strongest performance
